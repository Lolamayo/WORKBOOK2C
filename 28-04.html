28/04: Sisters with Transistors



The theremin



This movie is devoted to the unsung pioneers of electronic music, such as Clara Rockmore, who helped to popularize Russian physicist Leon Theremin's an electronic instrument that could be played without any physical contact in the 1920s. Players stood in front of a box and waved their hands over antennas, summoning otherworldly sounds seemingly from thin air.
