25/04: ARDUINO // BLINK



Today in tut we were introduced to the Arduino kits, and downloaded the Arduino IDE application.



Then, we made our first Arduino circuit.. BLINK.
This arduino project is the absolute most beginner-level project. A button is connected to a LED bulb by a resistor and four wires so that upon pressing the button, the bulb illuminates.
