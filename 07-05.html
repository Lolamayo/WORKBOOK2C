07/04: Arduino Experimentation



1.I played around with arduino capacitance, adjusting (raising) the threshold of the serial monitor to make the light illuminate not when the wire is plugged into the plant, but only when I touched the plant, grounding the circuit. The serial monitor reading differed based on where on the plant I touched it.

2.I followed a basic circuit using an active buzzer, and the Arduino Tone Library. When I ran the code, the buzzer played a simple harmony. The harmony does not sound extremely pleasant, as active buzzers are only able to produce simple, squawking sounds. Passive buzzers are able to produce nicer sounds, and there is a broader range of projects and circuits possible with them. Additionally, speakers placed on a bread board can play any music downloaded on the computer.
